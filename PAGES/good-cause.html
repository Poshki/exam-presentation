Save the planet?!

We all know that we as individuals cannot save the entire planet by ourselves – but at the very minimum if we all just do a little bit in our everyday life, we can help to protect the planet just a bit more than now. Buying products here with us at TINC you will always know that no matter what product you choose – it is sustainable and environmentally friendly. Furthermore a lot of our products actually donate 10 % of the cost to charity. This means you do not pay more but some of the money goes directly to this cause. Even little things like your toothbrush and toothpaste can help towards the planet. Or the cottonpads you use to rinse your face off every night – why not use cotton pads that are reusable instead of one-time use pads? We are not saying you have to go all in but even just the little things that you have to do anyways during your day – it’s minimal effort for you to change this and it gives maximum benefits for the planet. So why not is all we ask. Please never hesitate to come in to the shop and talk to us about these subjects. We are very invested and always love to either help or just have a friendly chat about the situation and how we as single persons can help to do just a small change in the world.
